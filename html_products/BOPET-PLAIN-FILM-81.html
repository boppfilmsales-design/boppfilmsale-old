BOPET PLAIN FILM 
BOPET film (biaxially oriented Polyester films) featurines good thermal stability and tenacity; low air and water permeability; applicable to vacuum aluminum plating, compound package, cooking bag manufacture, etc. 
Featuring : 
Having the good adhesion with ink and coating, can be used for multi-color printings with good flatness. Is mainly used in heat-sealing bags of frozen food, industrial products and cosmetics packaging.
Specification:
thickness: 12 microns-25 microns, 75 microns-150 microns ,185 microns-380 microns, 500 microns
width :200-1200MM 
length : 500m ,1000m ,1500m,4000m,6000m,9000m,12000m
