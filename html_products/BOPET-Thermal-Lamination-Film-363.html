Lamination film is pre-glue on the based of the PET glossy film. It is used for the cover of the books, magazines, and advertisement paper, etc. (It is lamination on the covers or the paper after the hot laminating machine. ) 

The main characteristics of PET Lamianting film: 
1) It doesn't include solvent adhesive, which can protect the print color from changing. 
2) The adhesive effect is superior finish & gloss, which is much better than that with the solvent adhesive. 
3) Time saving, you don't need to prepare the glue beforehand. 
4) It can also be used on the traditional lamination machine. And it would not cause tunneling and swelling. 

Size: Any specification can be produced ranging from identify card to A2 paper. 
Thickness: According to the clients' requirements, any composition of the total thickness from 32 micron to 250 micron can be produced, including PET+EVA.
