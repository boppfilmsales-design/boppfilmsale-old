Heat Sealable BOPP Film

Heat sealable BOPP film production line consists of casting sheet extrusion, biaxial orientation, after treatment, rolling, cutting, and automatic control. It can produce BOPP films of various specifications and stable dimensions, with good mechanical and electrical performances, good heat resistance, cold resistance and gastightness.

FILM DESCRIPTION:          
Two sides heat sealable; one side treated co extruded biaxial oriented polypropylene transparent film, suitable for:    
Rotogravure and flexographic printing.        
Lamination with similar films, paper or other films.        
Horizontal and vertical packaging machines (HFFS – VFFS).        
General over wrapping purposes.        
           
CHARACTERISTICS          
Two sides heat sealable with excellent hot tack.        
High transparency.          
Excellent ink adhesion on treated side.        
High resistance to oil, grease, chemicals, puncture and abrasion.        
Good barrier to moisture, odors and gases.        
           
APPLICATIONS:          
Food packaging:          
Biscuit, pasta, snacks, confectionary, chips, tea, and bakery.        
Non–food packaging:          
Stationary, textiles, gifts, and flowers.
